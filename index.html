My Life Comix!


My Life Comix is a comic founded by me in Primary 5, inspired by teachers. The main character is a student named Leo and a crazy teacher named Ms Bing. Through science and craziness, we travel through time (not always) and have whopping adventures!
Since 2013, we transitioned from paper to computer. Since 2014, we started making books and videos too. Aside from the weekly main comic strip, we also have adventure stories such as Realities.
